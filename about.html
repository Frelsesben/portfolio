About

This is me with my dog, Keanu. We live with a man named August (my boyfriend) and a cat named Olga, in a small town close to where I grew up. Here, we enjoy the quietness and nearby green sceneries.
I've always been fond of animals, and went vegan in 2018 (a big drive for this was watching this video).
Since 2020, I have also been part of a number of non-profits, advocating for animal rights. Today, I'm mostly active in Dyrenes Alliance, where I join in on activities such as handing out flyers and/or samples of plantbased food.
Besides this (and coding, of course!) I enjoy playing squash and dancing like nobody's watching.
